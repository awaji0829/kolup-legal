Privacy Policy

Effective: April 9, 2026 · Last updated: April 9, 2026

Color Codi ("the Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information the Service processes and how.

1. Information We Collect

The Service does not collect or store any personal information on its servers. There is no sign-up, login, or user account. We do not request your name, email, phone number, date of birth, or any other personally identifying information.

2. Information Processed On Your Device

To provide core functionality, the following information is processed on your device or transmitted to third-party APIs. None of it is stored on our servers.

  • Location (coordinates): With your permission, the app reads your device location and sends the coordinates to the Open-Meteo weather API (open-meteo.com) in order to recommend outfits suitable for current weather. We do not store your location.
  • Camera and Photos: The app accesses your camera and photo library only when you choose to take or pick a photo for color extraction. Images are processed on-device only and are not uploaded or stored anywhere.
  • Preferences: Settings you enter (gender, style keywords, clothing categories) are stored only in local device storage (AsyncStorage) and are removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. The Service communicates with the following external services to provide functionality:

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — weather data (receives location coordinates)
  • Cloudflare Pages (color-codi-assets.pages.dev) — CDN for outfit assets

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not retain any personal data on our servers. All local settings are deleted when you uninstall the app.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke location, camera, or photo permissions at any time through your iOS Settings. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or 14 in Korea).

8. Changes to This Policy

We will post any changes to this policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date above.
